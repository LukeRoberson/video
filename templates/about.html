About This Site

This site is a collection of videos and resources from jw.org.


Feature Overview:
  • Watch videos directly on the site, and track which ones you've already seen
  • Browse videos by topic, speaker, or bible character. Topics align with those on jw.org.
  • Dig deeper by browsing Bible characters, scriptures, speakers, locations, and tags
  • Search for videos by keyword and other criteria
  • Track videos in progress
  • Curated collections of videos based on themes
  • Video subtitles
  • No special app required. Just use the website on any device

Privacy

This website does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users. No cookies or tracking technologies are used.

All video content is embedded from jw.org; and no user data is transmitted to this site. This site is not affiliated with jw.org. It does not distribute any video content.
